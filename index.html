Welcome to the TriPed Project

The TriPed is a new three legged Robot, in development as a cooperation betweeb the Proaut lab, he aries lab and the Institute of Medical Device Technology at the University of Stuttgart.

The System uses a new kind of decentralized approach where each leg is independent from the rest and they need to cooperate, like humans do, by reading and responding to their subtle cues
